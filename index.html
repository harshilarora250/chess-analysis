Magnus Chess Analysis

A free, open, browser-based chess analysis tool powered by Stockfish 16 via WebAssembly — the world's strongest open-source chess engine. No sign-up, no server, no cost.

  • ♟ Full legal move validation
  • ⚡ Real-time Stockfish evaluation
  • 📋 PGN import & export
  • 🔁 Move navigation & replay
  • 🧮 Multi-line analysis
  • 📐 FEN import/export

All analysis runs entirely in your browser. Your games never leave your device.
